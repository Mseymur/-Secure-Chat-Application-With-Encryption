End-to-End Encryption

SecureChat uses end-to-end encryption to ensure that only you and your chat partner can read your messages. No one else, not even us, can access your conversations.

How Encryption Keys Work

Each chat has a unique encryption key. To chat with someone:

  1. Create a new chat and share the encryption key with your partner
  2. Your partner uses this key to join your chat
  3. Messages are automatically encrypted and decrypted
Important: Always share encryption keys through secure channels, not in the chat itself.

Security Tips

  • Never share your encryption keys publicly
  • Use a strong, unique password for your account
  • Log out when using shared devices
  • Keep your browser and system updated
